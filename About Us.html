About Us

Who We Are

Goingwad Projects (Pty) Ltd was founded in 2014 and registered by Mr. Jacob Tsholofelo Ramodingwana on the 30/04/2015. Ramodingwana is a qualified electrician and an experience welder. The main purpose of forming this Company, is for the supplying of the above stated services (Electrical, steelwork and Building projects) to the surrounding Local people, Businesses ,Government and Munipality.

Our Mission

We are a customer Focused Project Management company, We capitalize on use of management principles:

  • Directing and coordinating resources
  • Scope and time management
  • Quality management
  • Cost control
  • Participation that will ensure that the goals of the project is achieved

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a preferred service provider in the management programmes and projects in various disciplines / fields based on capabilities of the Company.
